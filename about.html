Our story

Dallas’s Garage Door Repair Specialists

To provide fast, honest, and high-quality garage door service to every Dallas homeowner and business.

RidgeForce Max Garage Door's Repair was founded in 2005 by a team of experienced technicians who saw a need for reliable, customer-focused service in Dallas. Starting with a single truck, we built a reputation for showing up on time, diagnosing problems accurately, and fixing them right the first time. Over the years, we've grown to a fleet of service vehicles serving all of Dallas and surrounding areas.

Today, we remain family-owned and operated. Every technician is background-checked, trained, and equipped with the best tools and parts. We treat every home like our own, and it shows in our repeat business and referrals. Whether it's a broken spring or a full door replacement, we're here to help.
