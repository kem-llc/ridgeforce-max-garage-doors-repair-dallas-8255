Our story

Your Local Garage Door Experts in Dallas

To provide Dallas homeowners and businesses with prompt, professional, and affordable garage door services backed by integrity and craftsmanship.

RidgeForce Max Garage Door's Repair was founded on the belief that garage door service should be straightforward and reliable. Our founder, a Dallas native with years of experience in the industry, saw too many customers overcharged for subpar work. He built a company focused on honest communication, fair pricing, and skilled workmanship — values that still drive us today.

Since opening our doors, we've grown from a one-truck operation to a trusted team serving all of Dallas and surrounding areas. Every technician is trained to handle any brand or issue, from simple adjustments to complex installations. We treat every home like our own, ensuring your garage door operates safely and looks great.
