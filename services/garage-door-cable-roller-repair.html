Garage Door Cable & Roller Repair

Replace frayed cables and worn rollers for smooth movement.

Schedule this service

Overview

Damaged cables or rollers can cause jerky operation and safety hazards. We replace cables with aircraft-grade wire and install nylon or steel rollers for quiet, smooth travel.

What's included

  • Eliminates door jerking and noise
  • Extends life of springs and opener
  • Improves security and alignment

How it works

1

Common questions

How often should rollers be replaced?

Every 5-7 years, or sooner if noisy or worn.

What causes cable fraying?

Rust, misalignment, or age. Regular maintenance prevents sudden breaks.
