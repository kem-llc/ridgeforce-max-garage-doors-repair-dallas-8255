Garage Door Installation

Professional installation of new garage doors for style and security.

Overview

Upgrade your home with a new garage door from top brands like Clopay, Amarr, and Wayne Dalton. We handle everything from removal of your old door to precise installation, including tracks, springs, openers, and weatherstripping. Choose from steel, wood, or composite designs.

What's included

  • Enhances home curb appeal and value
  • Improves insulation and security
  • Includes warranty on door and installation

How it works

1

Consultation

2

3

Installation

We install the new door, tracks, springs, and opener, then test for smooth operation.

Common questions

How long does a full installation take?

Typically 4-6 hours for a standard single or double door.

What insulation level should I choose?

For attached garages, R-value 12-16 is recommended. For detached, R-value 6-8 is sufficient.
