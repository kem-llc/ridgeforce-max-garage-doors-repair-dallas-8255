Garage Door Spring Repair

Expert torsion and extension spring replacement for smooth, safe operation.

Overview

Broken springs are the most common garage door issue. Our technicians replace both torsion and extension springs using high-cycle, oil-tempered steel springs that last longer and operate quieter. We ensure proper tension adjustment for balanced lifting.

What's included

  • Restores smooth door operation
  • Prevents further damage to opener
  • Increases safety with correct tension

How it works

1

Inspection

We assess spring type, size, and condition, checking for wear or damage.

2

Using precision tools, we safely remove old springs and install new high-quality ones.

3

Testing

We test balance and tension multiple times to ensure safe, quiet operation.

Common questions

How often should garage door springs be replaced?

Typically every 7-10 years depending on usage. Signs include difficulty opening or loud noises.

Is it dangerous to replace springs myself?

Yes, springs are under high tension and can cause serious injury. Always hire a professional.
